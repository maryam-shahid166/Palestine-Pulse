  • Palestinian Falafel

    Vegeterian

    Falafel (the national dish of Palestine) is deep-fried balls or patties made from chickpeas or fava beans, sometimes both, plus fresh herbs and spices.

  • Kabab Halabi

    Non-Vegeterian

    Kebab Halabi is one of the best grilled items, and not many places can make it as tasty as in the Middle East.

  • Maqluba

    Vegeterian

    Maqluba is a traditional Levantine dish that is popular across Palestine. It has meat, rice, and fried vegetables placed in a pot which is flipped upside down when served, hence the name.

  • Qidreh

    Non-Vegeterian

    Qidreh is a dish made of rice and meat pieces cooked with cloves, garlic and cardamom.

  • Zibdieh

    Non-Vegeterian

    Zibdieh is a clay-pot dish of shrimp baked in a stew of olive oil, garlic, hot peppers, and peeled tomatoes.

  • Rummaniyeh

    Vegeterian

    Rummaniyeh is a classic Palestinian dish, a lentil eggplant stew with pomegranate molasses.
